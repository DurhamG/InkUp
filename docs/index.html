InkUp

A handwriting app for e-ink tablets.
Write naturally. See clean text appear as you go.

How it works

  • Just write. Your handwriting is recognized in real time and rendered as formatted text.
  • Strike through words to delete them. Draw an X to remove an entire line.
  • Draw a vertical line to insert a blank line above or below.
  • Underline a word to make it a heading.
  • Draw a dash before your text to create a bulleted list item.
  • Auto-scroll kicks in as you write toward the bottom.
  • Export as markdown to share your notes anywhere.
